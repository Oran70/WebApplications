About OROOTS

Who We Are

ORoots is dedicated to transforming fruit tree roots into unique framed artworks. Our work blends nature and art, revealing hidden beauty in organic forms.

Why We Do This

Every root tells a story shaped by time and nature. Instead of discarding roots, we preserve and frame them to showcase their elegance. ORoots aims to bring a touch of nature into people's homes while promoting sustainability.

Our Process

  1. Growing & Selecting: We cultivate fruit tree roots and harvest them at the right size.
  2. Preserving & Flattening: Roots are carefully dried and pressed to maintain their structure.
  3. Framing & Display: Each root is placed in an elevated frame, highlighting its unique form.
  4. Sharing & Selling: Artworks are showcased online and made available for purchase.
